Progress Outcome Evaluation Tool

Anonymous crowdsourced evaluations matching Digital Technologies resources to Progress Outcomes.

{% endblock page_heading %} {% block content_container %}

The Progress Outcome Evaluation Tool has been developed to anonymously crowdsource information of how Digital Technologies resources relate to the Progress Outcomes, and to see how this data changes over time as students and teachers become more familiar with the revised Technology Curriculum.

Survey features include:

  • Resources are randomly selected from a set based on your selected year levels taught.
  • Ability to view distributions of all submissions, compared to your submission.
  • Ability to add feedback to your answer.
