Progress Outcome Evaluation Tool

Anonymous crowdsourced evalutions matching Digital Technology resources to progress outcomes.

The progress outcome evaluation tool has been developed to anonymously crowdsource information of where teachers how Digital Technology resources relate to progress outcomes, and to see how this data changes over time.

Survey features include:

  • Resources are randomly selected from a set based on your teaching area.
  • Ability to view distributions of all submissions, compared to your submission.
  • Ability to add feedback to your answer.
  • All submissions are anonymous.
